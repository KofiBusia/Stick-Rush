    How to Play Stick Rush

    🏃 Sprint Dash

    Tap or click as FAST as you can to make your stickman run. First to the finish line wins! Use Space or ArrowRight key on desktop.

    🦘 Jump Fever

    Tap to jump over obstacles! Survive for 20 seconds. Collect coins for bonus points. Double-tap for a double jump! Speed increases over time.

    🎯 Reflex Blaster

    Tap the glowing circles before they disappear! Build up combo streaks for mega bonus points. The faster you tap — the higher your score!

    📚 Brain Challenge (Q&A)

    After the 3 mini-games, answer 3 questions — Math and English Grammar. You have 15 seconds per question. Correct answers give bonus points!

    💰 Pricing

    Round 1 is 100% FREE! Rounds 2 and beyond cost GHS 5 each. You can pay with MTN Mobile Money, Vodafone Cash, or AirtelTigo Money.

    👕 Costumes

    Unlock new costumes by scoring points, winning mini-games, answering questions correctly, and completing rounds. Costumes go: Common → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Mythic!
