🏃 Stick Rush — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Manny Harry Games ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Stick Rush mobile application ("the App").

We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children. This policy complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

---

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Stick Rush does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Contacts or social media information
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

2. Data Stored on Your Device Only

The App stores the following data locally on your device only using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server:

  • Your chosen display name (nickname) and age group
  • Game scores and leaderboard entries
  • Costume selections and unlocked items
  • Round unlock status

You can delete all this data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or using the "Clear My Scores" button inside the app.

3. Internet Access

Stick Rush requests internet permission on Android for potential future online features. However, the current version of the app is fully offline and does not make any network requests, send data to servers, or communicate with the internet during gameplay.

4. Children's Privacy

Stick Rush is designed for children ages 8–16. We take children's privacy very seriously:

  • We do not collect personal information from children
  • We do not show third-party advertisements
  • We do not include social features or chat
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not require account creation or registration

5. In-App Purchases

The App includes optional in-app purchases (round unlocks for GHS 5 each). These payments are processed through the device's mobile money system (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo). We do not collect or store any payment information within the app.

6. Third-Party Services

Stick Rush does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or social media SDKs. No third party receives any data from this app.

7. Permissions Used

The App requests the following Android permission:

  • INTERNET — Required by the Capacitor framework. The current app version does not use internet connectivity.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted over the internet, your information is protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
