## Full Description (4000 chars max)

🏃 **STICK RUSH** — Ghana's Ultimate Stickman Party Game!

Play 3 epic mini-games, answer educational questions and compete on the leaderboard! Perfect for kids ages 8–16 across West Africa.

---

### 🎮 3 MINI-GAMES
**🏃 Sprint Dash** — Race your stickman against the CPU! Tap as fast as you can to charge ahead. Who crosses the finish line first?

**🦘 Jump Fever** — Dodge obstacles in this endless runner! Time your jumps perfectly, collect coins and beat the clock. Double-jump for extra height!

**🎯 Reflex Blaster** — Tap glowing circles before they vanish! Build combos for massive bonus points. How fast are your reflexes?

---

### 📚 LEARN WHILE YOU PLAY
After every round, answer 3 educational questions:
- **Mathematics:** Fractions, percentages, algebra, geometry
- **English:** Grammar, vocabulary, comprehension

Three difficulty tiers based on your age (8–10, 11–13, 14–16). Correct answers = BONUS POINTS!
